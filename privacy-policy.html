Privacy Policy for Flow Finance

Last Updated: May 1, 2026

Flow Finance ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you use Flow Finance, we may collect:

  • Account Information: Email address and name when you sign in with Google
  • Financial Data: Transaction records, budgets, categories, and financial goals you create within the app
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: App features used, time spent in app, interaction patterns
  • Analytics Data: App performance, crash reports, and error logs
  • Advertising Data: Ad impressions, clicks, and interactions (via Google AdMob)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • App Functionality: To provide core features like transaction tracking, budget management, and financial reports
  • Data Synchronization: To sync your data across devices using Firebase Cloud Firestore
  • Authentication: To verify your identity and secure your account
  • Analytics: To understand app usage and improve user experience
  • Advertising: To display relevant ads through Google AdMob
  • App Improvement: To fix bugs, improve performance, and develop new features

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Where Your Data is Stored

  • Local Storage: Financial data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database
  • Cloud Storage: When signed in, data is backed up to Firebase Cloud Firestore (Google Cloud Platform)
  • Location: Cloud data is stored on Google's secure servers

3.2 Security Measures

  • Data encryption in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Firebase Authentication for secure user access
  • Local device security (device lock, biometric authentication if enabled)
  • Regular security updates and patches

4. Third-Party Services

Flow Finance integrates with the following third-party services:

4.1 Google Services

  • Google Sign-In: For authentication (Privacy Policy)
  • Firebase Authentication: For user management
  • Firebase Cloud Firestore: For data synchronization
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements (Privacy Policy)

4.2 AdMob and Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Ad interaction data
  • Device and network information

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings (Google Ad Settings on Android).

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share data only in these circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing
  • Service Providers: With Google/Firebase for app functionality
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale

6. Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data we have about you
  • Export: Download your data in CSV or PDF format
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Correct: Update or correct your information
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics or personalized ads

How to Exercise Your Rights

  • Export Data: Use the "Export as CSV" or "Export as PDF" feature in Settings → Reports
  • Delete Account: Go to Settings → Delete Account
  • Clear Local Data: Go to Settings → Clear Data
  • Sign Out: Go to Settings → Sign Out

7. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Local Data: Remains on device until you clear it or uninstall the app
  • Backup Data: Cloud backups are deleted when you delete your account

8. Children's Privacy

Flow Finance is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us to have it removed.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country. By using Flow Finance, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries that may have different data protection laws than your country of residence.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an in-app notification for significant changes

Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Your Consent

By using Flow Finance, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
